Flank Steak

Description of Flank Steak

Flank steak is a cut of beef steak taken from the abdominal muscles of the cow, located just behind the plate and in front of the rear quarter. It is a long, flat cut with a significant grain, and is known for its bold flavor and chewiness.

Ingredients

  • Flank Steak
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Soy Sauce
  • Red Wine Vinegar
  • Lemon Juice
  • Worcestershire sauce
  • Dijon mustard
  • garlic
  • ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Whisk items
  3. Pour marinade over flank steak
  4. Refrigerate for 2 to 6 hours
  5. Preheat on grill for medium-high heat
  6. Remove steak from marinade
  7. Cook steak on preheated grill
  8. Remove from grill and let rest for 5 minutes
